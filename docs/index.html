Game instructions ▼

The game consists of 4 dice.

You should try to guess the sum of the first 3 dice before throwing them all. If your guess turns out to be lower than or equal (<=) to the right answer, your score will be incremented by the guessed number multiplied by the fourth dice.

You have 10 rounds to increase your score.
